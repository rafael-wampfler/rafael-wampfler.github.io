abstract: >
  We present a comprehensive platform for creating, deploying, and operating interactive AI character experiences. The platform integrates a full pipeline from perception to animation: real-time speech recognition, knowledge-grounded dialogue management with personality-consistent response generation, multimodal dialog act classification, and expressive data-driven facial animation with gesture synthesis. The system is designed for scalable deployment across scientific, medical, and public-facing contexts, and is demonstrated through Digital Einstein — an embodied conversational agent that has reached thousands of visitors globally at scientific conferences, technology summits, and public events. We describe the architectural choices, engineering tradeoffs, and lessons learned from sustained real-world operation.
