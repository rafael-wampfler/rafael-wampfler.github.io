abstract: >
  We present a dynamic cognitive framework for steering narrative agents in interactive digital entertainment. The framework enables agents to pursue coherent, goal-directed story arcs while remaining responsive to emergent player actions and choices. Using belief-desire-intention (BDI) representations combined with large language model generation, narrative agents dynamically update their internal goals, beliefs, and story intentions in response to player input, balancing narrative coherence with interactive agency. We evaluate the framework in a series of multi-turn interactive storytelling scenarios, demonstrating that agents guided by the framework maintain more coherent narratives and generate more satisfying story outcomes compared to unguided baselines.
