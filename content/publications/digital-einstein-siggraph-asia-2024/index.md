abstract: >
  We present an immersive conversational experience with Digital Einstein, an embodied AI agent that brings the historical figure of Albert Einstein to life. This Emerging Technologies demonstration describes the technical integration of a physical animatronic display system with a real-time AI pipeline encompassing speech recognition, knowledge-grounded dialogue management, personality-consistent response generation, and synchronized facial animation. The paper details the architectural decisions, latency optimizations, and real-world deployment lessons from the SIGGRAPH Asia 2024 installation in Tokyo, Japan, where thousands of visitors engaged in live conversations with the Digital Einstein system.
